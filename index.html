Laser Lipo

Laser Lipo using square pads is a minimally invasive cosmetic procedure that uses a laser to melt away fat under the skin. The most common areas for laser lipo are the stomach area, love handles, thighs and arms.

Ultrasonic Cavitation

Ultrasonic cavitation uses ultra sound technology to break apart fat cells. Ultrasound waves are sent deep into the layers of the skin causing a disruptive vibration, expelling fat cells from the body.
